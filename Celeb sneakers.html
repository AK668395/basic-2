Bollywood Icons: Luxury & Streetwear

Ranveer Singh

The Style King

Known for his eccentric and high-energy style, Ranveer is a true sneakerhead. His closet features the boldest drops, including rare **Adidas Yeezy** colorways, **Off-White x Nike Air Jordans**, and luxury chunky runners. He sets the bar for experimental footwear.

#LimitedEdition #Hypebeast

Deepika Padukone

The Elegant Minimalist

Deepika opts for sophisticated comfort. Her preference leans towards luxury neutral sneakers like those from **Balenciaga** or premium lines from **Adidas**. She masterfully blends designer elegance with casual athleisure looks.

#LuxuryStreet #CleanVibes

Shah Rukh Khan (SRK)

The Timeless Icon

The 'Badshah' of Bollywood favors high-end, classic European brands. He is frequently spotted in the pre-distressed style of **Golden Goose Superstar** kicks and the minimalist luxury of **Common Projects** sneakers.

#DistressedCool #ItalianCraft

Hip-Hop Royalty: Unmatched Collections

Badshah

The 1000+ Collection

Badshah is known to own over 1,000 pairs. The crown jewel? The ultra-rare **Nike Air Yeezy 2 (Red October)**, which he reportedly bought for 6 Lakhs and is now valued up to 22 Lakhs. He is the undisputed king of Indian sneaker culture.

#RedOctober #GrailStatus

Raftaar

High-Fashion Hype

Raftaar is the "God of Hypebeasts" among Indian rappers, often sporting luxury sneakers over classics. His notable pairs include the chunky **Versace Chain Reactions**, **Dolce & Gabbana Super Kings**, and **Louis Vuitton LV Trainer Hi-Tops** (designed by Virgil Abloh).

#VirgilAbloh #ChunkySolidity

MC Stan

The Trend Setter

A major force in new-age Indian Hip-Hop, MC Stan embodies modern street fashion. He integrates iconic American streetwear silhouettes, often seen wearing high-top and bulky styles, showcasing collaborations and limited edition drops like the **Nike Air Force 1**.

#StreetAesthetic #NewWave
